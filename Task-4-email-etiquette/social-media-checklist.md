# Responsible Social Media Use – College Student Checklist
## ✅ Do's

Keep your profiles clean and professional — future employers do check
Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be okay with anyone seeing it
Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas
Use social media to network, learn, and build your personal brand

## ❌ Don'ts

Don't share personal information like your address, phone number, or location publicly
Don't post anything in anger or frustration — you can always delete a draft, not a screenshot
Don't engage with or spread misinformation
Don't ignore or bully others online — digital actions have real consequences
Don't overshare your daily routine — it's a safety risk
