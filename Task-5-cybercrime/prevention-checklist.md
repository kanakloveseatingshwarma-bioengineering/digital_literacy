# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

1. Never click on links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email without verifying the sender first

2. Always check the URL of a website before entering any personal information — look for https:// and watch out for spelling mistakes
   
3. Never share your OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or a government office

4. 💳 UPI Safety: Remember that receiving money on UPI never requires you to enter your PIN — if someone asks you to, it is a scam

5. 💳 UPI Safety: Set a daily transaction limit on your UPI app so that even if you are targeted, the loss is limited

6. Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible

7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, shopping, or logging into any important account

8. Regularly check your bank statements and UPI transaction history for anything suspicious

9. Do not save your card details on websites or apps you do not fully trust

10. Report anything suspicious immediately at 🌐 cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline 📞 1930
